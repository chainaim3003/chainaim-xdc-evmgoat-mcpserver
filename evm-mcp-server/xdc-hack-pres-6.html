The Standards Crisis: Why RWA Tokenization Fails

No ACTUS Financial Standards

Central banks use ACTUS for financial modeling, but zero RWA platforms support it. This prevents institutional adoption and regulatory acceptance of tokenized financial instruments.

Missing GLEIF Identity Integration

Global Legal Entity Identifier system is mandatory for institutional finance, yet no tokenization platform integrates vLEI. This creates compliance nightmares.

No DCSA Shipping Compliance

Digital Container Shipping Association standards govern $14T in global trade, but Bills of Lading tokenization ignores DCSA schemas. This blocks trade finance adoption.

Absent BPMN Process Validation

Business Process Model Notation is the global standard for workflow verification, yet no platform can prove business process execution for delivery vs promise validation.

MLETR Non-Compliance

Model Law for Electronic Transmission of Records governs digital trade documents, but current platforms use simple hashes instead of full MLETR compliance.

ISO 20022 Messaging Gap

Financial messaging standard adopted by central banks globally, but RWA platforms cannot integrate with existing banking infrastructure due to messaging incompatibility.
